About Coins

The story behind the ultimate cryptocurrency portfolio tracker

Our Mission

At Coins, we believe that managing your cryptocurrency investments should be simple, secure, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide the most intuitive and powerful portfolio tracking experience on iOS, helping both newcomers and experienced traders make informed decisions about their digital assets.

We're passionate about cryptocurrency and understand the challenges investors face when trying to track their diverse portfolios across multiple exchanges and wallets. That's why we created Coins - to bring all your investments together in one beautiful, easy-to-use app.

Privacy First

Your financial privacy is our top priority. Unlike other portfolio trackers that require accounts and store your data on their servers, Coins operates entirely on your device. We don't collect any personal information, and we never see your portfolio data.

All your investment information stays securely on your iPhone or iPad, with optional iCloud sync to keep your data private but accessible across your devices. No sign-ups, no data mining, just pure privacy.

Built for iOS

Coins is designed from the ground up specifically for iOS, taking full advantage of the platform's capabilities. We use native iOS technologies to deliver smooth performance, beautiful animations, and seamless integration with iOS features like Dark Mode, widgets, and Apple Watch support.

Our app follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines to provide an experience that feels natural and intuitive to iOS users. Every interaction is carefully crafted to be fast, responsive, and delightful.

Continuous Innovation

The cryptocurrency market never sleeps, and neither do we. Our team continuously works to add new features, support for additional exchanges, and improvements based on user feedback. We're committed to keeping Coins at the forefront of portfolio tracking technology.

Regular updates ensure that you always have access to the latest features and the most accurate market data. We listen to our users and implement the features that matter most to the cryptocurrency community.

Community Driven

Coins is built by cryptocurrency enthusiasts, for cryptocurrency enthusiasts. We understand the needs of the crypto community because we're part of it. Our development roadmap is guided by real user feedback and the evolving needs of cryptocurrency investors.

Join our growing community of users who trust Coins to track their cryptocurrency investments. Whether you're a HODLer, day trader, or just getting started with crypto, Coins has the tools you need to succeed.
